I put this resource together after realizing that the average reef aquarium hobbyist is generally unaware of the potential for exposure to palytoxin from their reef tanks.

What you will learn

This site is to serve as a general resource for learning:

  • Information about this toxin
  • What the animals generally look like in your reef aquarium
  • What personal protective equipment one should use to minimize exposure
  • How to document the animals in your tank for health care professionals in case you are exposed to palytoxin or other venemous animals in your care
  • What to do when you are exposed and what information you should take with you in case you need to head to the emergency room
  • Examples of hobbyist exposure
  • Online resources where you can learn more about the animals in your care

If this website helps just one person, then I've done my job.
